Drinks Ordering Rules

  1. Minimum age for ordering certain drinks is 18 years.
  2. Please check the ingredients before placing an order.
  3. Orders cannot be changed or canceled after confirmation
  4. Drinks will be served within 30 minutes of ordering.
  5. Each customer can order a maximum of 3 drinks at a time .
  6. Outside food and drinks are not allowed.
  7. Please inform the staff about any allergies before ordering.
  8. Wasting drinks is discouraged; order responsibly.
  9. Some drinks may contain caffeine or alcohol order accordingly.
  10. Happy Hour discounts apply only to selected drinks.
